####Prefix Notation
- Where the operator is enclosed in parentheses and preprends the two binary arguments.

```
Prelude> (+) 3 5
8
Prelude> (++) [3, 4] [5]
[3,4,5]
Prelude> (+) 3 ((-) 5 2)
6
```
